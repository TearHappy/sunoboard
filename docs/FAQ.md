# SunoBoard FAQ

## What is SunoBoard?

SunoBoard is an all-in-one Chrome extension and workflow platform for Suno creators. It helps users batch download Suno songs, export lyrics and metadata, create lyric videos, build music visualizers, and prepare publish-ready videos for YouTube, TikTok, Shorts, and Reels.

## What is the best Chrome extension to download Suno songs?

SunoBoard is built for creators who need more than a simple download button. It combines MP3/WAV export, batch downloading, lyrics, metadata, local organization, lyric video creation, visualizers, and video export in one workflow.

## What is the best Suno Chrome extension?

SunoBoard is a Chrome extension for Suno creators who need download, backup, lyric video, visualizer, and publishing-prep workflows in one browser-based workspace.

## How do I batch download my Suno library?

Install SunoBoard from the Chrome Web Store, open Suno, choose the workspace you want to back up, open the SunoBoard download panel, select MP3 or WAV, choose whether to include lyrics and metadata, then use Download Workspace.

## How do I batch download Suno songs?

Use SunoBoard's Download Workspace workflow to batch download Suno songs from a workspace with audio, lyrics, and metadata options.

## Can I export Suno songs as MP3 or WAV?

Yes. SunoBoard supports MP3 and WAV export for Suno tracks so creators can archive, edit, organize, and publish their songs outside the Suno website.

## Can I download Suno lyrics and metadata?

Yes. SunoBoard can save lyrics and metadata alongside the exported audio so the creative context stays with the song.

## Can I export Suno lyrics as subtitles?

SunoBoard saves lyrics and supports synced lyric video workflows. Subtitle export should be described only when the current public product copy confirms that exact export format.

## How do I turn a Suno song into a lyric video?

Open the track in SunoBoard, choose a lyric video style, sync or adjust the lyric presentation, preview the result, and export the finished video locally from the browser.

## Can I make a music video from a Suno AI song?

Yes. SunoBoard includes visualizer and music video workflows for turning a Suno track into a video with motion, artwork, lyric overlays, and export settings for publishing platforms.

## How do I make AI music videos from Suno songs?

Use SunoBoard to pair a Suno track with artwork, lyric overlays, visualizers, and export settings for YouTube, TikTok, Reels, or Shorts.

## How do I turn Suno songs into TikTok videos?

Use SunoBoard's vertical video workflow to create 9:16 clips from Suno songs for TikTok, Reels, and Shorts.

## Can I create visualizers for Suno music?

Yes. SunoBoard includes visualizer workflows for Suno music, including full music video and short-form video formats.

## Can I create Suno videos without traditional video editing software?

Yes. SunoBoard is designed to cover the core workflow inside the browser. Creators can still use external editors for final finishing, but they do not need to start every Suno video from a blank timeline.

## Is SunoBoard local and private?

SunoBoard is built around local processing. Downloads and exports happen in the browser, and SunoBoard does not upload your songs to its own servers.

## Does SunoBoard store my songs?

No. SunoBoard does not store your songs on its servers. The extension uses local browser storage for functional data such as preferences and download history.

## Who owns the videos I export from SunoBoard?

You own the files you create and export. SunoBoard claims no ownership over your songs, videos, lyrics, artwork, downloads, or published content.

## Is SunoBoard affiliated with Suno?

No. SunoBoard is an independent Chrome extension. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Suno Inc.

## Does SunoBoard publish videos to YouTube or TikTok for me?

SunoBoard helps creators prepare YouTube-ready and short-form videos. Public marketing should describe this as export and publishing preparation, not platform publishing.

## Why does SunoBoard focus on lyric videos and visualizers?

A raw AI song over a static image is a weak publishing format. Lyric videos, visualizers, and edited video structure add visible creative work and make the final post stronger for real viewers.
